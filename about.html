About

Our core values of safety, sustainability, and craftsmanship underpin every project we undertake. We are committed to utilizing cutting-edge technology and eco-friendly practices, ensuring that your project is not only built to last but also built to respect the environment.

History

We started Bob's Corporations in 1996 with a small team of workers, but we quickly grew to become one of the leading construction companies in the city. We specialize in all types of construction projects, from small home repairs to large commercial developments.

We're proud to say that Bob's Corporations has played a role in shaping the city of Horsens. We've built some of the city's most iconic landmarks, including the new City Hall, the renovated Museum of Art, and the Horsens Arena. We've also developed new residential neighborhoods and commercial districts.
